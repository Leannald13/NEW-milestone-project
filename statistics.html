Does this sound familiar?

Well, you aren't alone! So many of us face feelings of lonliness, sadness, anxiety and depression sometimes! The most important thing is to ask for help! Telling someone how you feel can make a big difference, and if you feel like there isn't anyone in your life you can talk to, there are so many charities and help lines that are always available to talk. Young Minds, Mind, NSPCC, etc.

We all need to do our part

The mental health crisis both globally and in the UK is real and it's urgent. We need to do more to support younger generations so that they develop coping strategies that they can take with them into adulthood.
