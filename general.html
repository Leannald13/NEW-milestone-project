I started presenting after coming runner-up at the BAFTA Kids Young Presenter competition. I present at everything from screenings to premiers on big channels such as Nickelodeon and the Disney Channel. For me, my biggest achievement so far has been to speak at The 2018 Children's Media Conference, where I spoke to people in the industry about how the media can positively support Mental Health in children and young people. My presenting gave me the confidence to start my own podcast; "It's good to talk". I talk to inspiriational people and celebrities about their stories and their own battles with mental health, body image and self-confidence. Follow me on social media and check me out on YouTube, Spotify and iTunes.

Many children and young people in the UK are struggling with mental health problems. I therefore decided to run a social media campaign called Harrison's Mental Health Campaign. The aim of this is to provide more support to children and young people by providing Mental Health Officers in Schools. The same way that each school has a nurse who is trained to care for the physical needs of a child, there is an urgent need for a trained professional to care for the mental health needs of children. I believe that if more services were available, the difficulties that children face in today's society, would be dealt with early on, preventing it from developing into a serious mental health disorder. It is reported that 1 in 4 people suffer from poor mental health at some point in their life. If there was more focus on early intervention, the youth of today would have the opportunity to develop strategies that they could carry into their adult lives, thus improving long term mental health. After all, children are the future.
