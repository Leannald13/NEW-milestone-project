I started presenting after coming runner-up at the BAFTA Kids Young Presenter competition. I present at everything from screenings to premiers on big channels such as Nickelodeon and the Disney Channel. For me, my biggest achievement so far has been to speak at The Children's Media Conference 2018 where I talked to people in the industry about how the media can positively support Mental Health in children and young people. My presenting then gave me the confidence to start my own podcast; "It's good to talk" where I talk to inspiriational people and celebrities about their stories and their own battles with mental health, body image and self-confidence. Follow me on social media and check me out on YouTube, Spotify and iTunes.

As a 13 year old with Aspergers and ADHD, I face daily challenges and require support from alot of people. Similarly, my peers and many children and young people in UK are struggling with mental health problems. I therefore decided to run a social media campaign called Harrison's Mental Health Campaign. The aim of this campaign is to provide more support to children and young people by providing Mental Health Officers in Schools to support us. This is a global issue which affects us all. Please sign my petition at the bottom of the page and help support me in fighting for the mental health and wellbeing of every child and young person in the UK.
