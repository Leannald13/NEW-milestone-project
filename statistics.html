What's the big picture?

  • Depression: 80,000 children
  • Anxiety: 1 in 6 children
  • 177 suicides in 2018 between 15-19 years old
  • 13% of children admit to self harming by the age of 16 years old.

You're not alone!

The most important thing we can do is to ask for help! Telling someone how you feel can make a big difference. If you feel like there isn't anyone in your life you can talk to, there are many charities and help lines that are always available to talk to, any day of the week:

  • Young Minds
  • Mind
  • NSPCC
  • Prince's Trust
