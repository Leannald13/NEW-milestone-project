"As a 13 year old with Aspergers and ADHD, I face daily challenges and require support from alot of people. I started to notice how many of my peers faced their own struggles from mental health problems, to cyber bulling, body shaming and learning disabilities I feel strongly that more support should be provided to children and young people, to build their self esteem and support their mental wellbeing."

What's the problem?

It is reported that approximately 1 in 8 children in the UK have a Mental Health Disorder.

What's my goal

To ensure that every child and young person in this country is provided with the support they need. No one should have to fight mental health alone.

How do I plan to do it?

As a young presenter, I am using my voice to raise awareness and improve services. Join my Mental Health Campaign to fight for the health and wellbeing of children and young people in the UK.
